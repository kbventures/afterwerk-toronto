Our Core Values

Live a better lifestyle

We care about your well being and "being healthy" encompasses many factors. We Promote an all-encompassing approach to living a healthy lifestyle including looking after our mental health, moving, being connected with others and most importantly; having fun!

Connect with good people

After Werk throws fun memorable events filled with amazing LGBTQ+ folks and allies where you can meet great friends, network, dance and maybe win prizes!

Sharing is caring

We love social media! Our social media platform share fun events as well as articles online to make you think and reflect - some to make you laugh.

Give Back

Giving back is an essential part of a strong thriving community, our mandate is to donate 5% of our profits to worthy LGBTQ+ charities.

After Werk

A brief history...

After Werk is a lifetstyle brand dedicated to being your best outside work. We enable connecting with others through fun events, practicing self-care with help from products in our online store. We proudly founded by LGBTQ+ folks for LGBTQ+ folks allies. After Werk started out as packed parties featuring incredible artists like "Canada Drag Race" superstars Priyanka Love(winner) and Boa the Drag Queen, as well as DJ Sofia Fly spinning house, throwbacks and Latin beats. We have evolved into a lifestyle brand and online shop which currently offers fitness and wellness products.

After Werk

A brief history...

"The After Werk lifestyle is one where you are comfortable in your own skin, where you reach out to others to give and get support, you thrive for more and most importantly you have fun! I've always truely enjoyed connecting folks and helping them achieve their goals. Being chained to a desk for decades affected my mental and physical health; I was always tired, overweight, battling depression and it popup__left me ffeeling like I was not having a meaningful impact on the world. I decided it was time to start a new chapter and it was time to tackle all my demons . After seeking professional support and putting in great work, I decided to evovle the After Werk Brand, and help others by sharing tools and wisdom I had learnt. I was dertmined to follow my dreams and build something that promotes community, self love and self growth. Having grown up in a different parts of Canada, I've mixed in maritime charm, B.C. buzz and Montreal fun to the organization. The After Werk Community is one where people lift themselves up and help each other set and achieve goals. After Werk was founded on the idea that connecting and engaging will help build a strong sense of community where folks will feel more comfortable expressing themselves, seeking support and grow as a result."

-Jean-Gilles Parisé
